== Description ==

contact form for your blog or web-site.

Adds a *Contact Us* button to the top right of your WordPress site. Click the button to pop up the Contact Form.

Contact Form features include:

+ Receive email notification when a form is completed
+ Easily customize the contact form
+ Add your own fields and text
+ Works on mobile devices
+ Reliable and secure (ask the 30000+ users)
+ SPAM free. No need for captcha.
+ Pop-up means no need to leave the page in order to send you a message
+ Foreign language support
+ Complete message history available for visitors and for the website administrator
+ Feedback Tool and Request a Call included
+ Optional live chat available

Free service includes 2 messages / day. See [www.userpulse.com](https://www.userpulse.com/securepages/create-account.jsp) for pricing.

Upon installation (and your next visit to your website), a userpulse.com account will be created for you to manage your settings and view a history of your messages. We'll email you confirmation details (to your WordPress.org email address), and forward completed contact forms. We may also send you occasional news and updates. We will never share your details with anyone else (see the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use on our website).


== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `contactusplus.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the UserPulse plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
